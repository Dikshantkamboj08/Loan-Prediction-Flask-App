About Loan Prediction System

The Loan Prediction System is a smart solution that helps financial institutions determine the eligibility of applicants for loans.

Users provide key details such as income, employment status, education, and credit history, which are analyzed by a trained machine learning model.

The system offers quick and accurate predictions, ensuring a fair and efficient loan approval process.

This tool is valuable for banks to automate decision-making and for applicants to get instant insights into their loan eligibility.
